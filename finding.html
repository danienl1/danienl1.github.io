User Interviews

User interviews were a significant part of my design and development. Through these interviews, I got a grasp of what various stakeholders would expect to see out of a professional portfolio. To seek out their perspective, I asked them serveral questions along the lines of what should be presented, what should be displayed, organization, structure, and depth.

Competitor Analysis

A competitor analysis is a strategical way to identify other competitors that affect your product, which is done by evaluating their strengths and weaknesses as compared to your own. For my portfolio, I reviewed mainly collaborative coding platforms, such as stackoverflow and github. This is because my field of study revolves around what they offer to the community, and to build off their strengths would benefit me.
